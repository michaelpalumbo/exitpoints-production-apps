Performer profile for Exit Points 70 on March 27 (Tickets at exitpoints.org): kimia farokhzad @kimia_farokhzad

Kimia Farokhzad is a Toronto-based Iranian singer-songwriter and multidisciplinary performer. She began her artistic journey in theatre in Tehran and started music at 17 as a vocalist, drawn to the emotional force of storytelling through sound.

Her work moves between pop, Iranian folk, free jazz, and experimental music, blending structured composition with improvisation and risk. Passionate about narrative and atmosphere, Kimia merges performance, movement, and music to create immersive experiences. Inspired by contemporary Persian poetry and her cross-cultural life, her current work explores identity, memory, and transformation through bold, genre-defying expression.

Performer profile for Exit Points 70 on March 27 (Tickets at exitpoints.org): Stephen Acosta @stephenacostaccpo

Stephen Acosta is musician and 2d animator from Mississauga Ontario. Stephen hosts a 2d animated music series called "the journey" both on youtube and live in Toronto. Stephen draws all his animations from scratch using early 2000's children's drawing softwares, such as tux paint and kid pix. His music stems from both the gospel, and his love for games he played growing up, such as Maplestory, Pokemon Mystery Dungeon, and freeze tag. The way these two elements come together are nothing short of odd, spidey sense tingling, but familiar. Stephen invites you to have an unfiltered look into his past and current mistakes through his animated music series called, once again, "the journey"

Chibu Victor Igwilo is a Toronto-based digital product designer, media producer, and vocalist whose work sits at the intersection of art, technology, and community storytelling. In addition to his work with studios and galleries across the country, he has launched a queer wellness brand aimed at improving the infrastructure that supports the queer community.
